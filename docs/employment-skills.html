Employment Services in Spokane

Spokane offers a robust network of employment resources connecting job seekers with career opportunities, resume assistance, interview preparation, and ongoing skills development. Many services specialize in supporting individuals in addiction recovery and those re-entering the workforce after incarceration.

Organization Address Phone Services Requirements
WorkSource Spokane – Career Center 130 S Arthur St, Spokane, WA 99202 (509) 324-9160 Job search assistance, resume help, career counseling, workshops, veteran services Open to all job seekers
Goodwill Industries of the Inland Northwest 130 S Arthur St, Spokane, WA 99202 (509) 458-5030 Employment services, vocational training, job placement, interview coaching Open to all; focus on barriers to employment
Pioneer Human Services Spokane 130 S Arthur St, Spokane, WA 99202 (509) 325-2358 Employment for formerly incarcerated, job training, placements, re-entry services Adults with criminal backgrounds
Division of Vocational Rehabilitation (DVR) 4407 N. Division St, Suite 315, Spokane, WA 99207 (509) 482-3044 Vocational rehabilitation, assistive technology, job accommodations, disability support Must have qualifying disability
SL Start – Re-Entry Employment 901 E 2nd Ave, Spokane, WA 99202 (509) 457-0677 Employment for people re-entering from corrections, housing support, life skills, mentorship Adults post-incarceration
