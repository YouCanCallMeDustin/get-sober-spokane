Addiction Treatment Spokane

Comprehensive Rehab and Treatment Facilities in Spokane, WA

Finding the Right Addiction Treatment in Spokane

Recognizing the need for help is the hardest step. Spokane offers a comprehensive network of addiction treatment centers equipped to handle varying severities of substance use disorders. From medically-supervised detox to long-term inpatient care, you can find the right treatment approach in Spokane, WA to give you the best chance at lasting recovery.

Levels of Care Available

Addiction isn't a one-size-fits-all disease, and neither is its treatment. Spokane facilities offer multiple levels of care:

  • Inpatient / Residential Rehab: You reside at the facility 24/7. This provides an immersive, structured environment removed from everyday triggers. Treatment includes individual therapy, group counseling, and medical supervision. Programs typically last 30, 60, or 90 days.
  • Partial Hospitalization Programs (PHP): Often a step down from inpatient treatment. Patients attend comprehensive treatment sessions at a hospital or clinic during the day (often 5-7 days a week) but return home or to a sober living facility at night.
  • Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP): Perfect for individuals who have completed inpatient treatment or have a mild dependency. IOP allows you to maintain work or school commitments while attending therapy sessions multiple times a week.
  • Standard Outpatient Care: Weekly individual or group therapy sessions focusing on relapse prevention and maintaining sobriety.

Therapies and Approaches

The best addiction treatment centers in Spokane utilize evidence-based therapies combined with holistic practices to treat the whole person:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Identifying and changing negative thought patterns.
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT): Focusing on emotional regulation and distress tolerance.
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT): Utilizing medications (like Suboxone or Methadone) alongside counseling to treat opioid or alcohol use disorders.
  • Dual Diagnosis Treatment: Simultaneously treating addiction and co-occurring mental health disorders like depression, anxiety, or PTSD.

Taking the Next Step

Choosing a treatment center is a deeply personal decision. Consider factors such as location, insurance acceptance, treatment philosophy, and specific amenities. We encourage you to reach out to local Spokane facilities or consult with a qualified addiction specialist to determine the best path forward.
