24/7 Crisis Services

If you or someone you know is in crisis, these resources are available around the clock, 365 days a year.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential 24/7 support. Connects to Spokane-area responders.

Call 988
Spokane Regional Crisis Line

24/7 local crisis intervention and mobile crisis team dispatch.

Call (877) 266-1818
Crisis Text Line

24/7 crisis support via text message for immediate help.

Text HOME to 741741

Other Health Services

  • Dental Care: CHAS Health and Providence offer dental services. Visit chas.org for locations.
  • Veterans' Health: Mann Grandstaff VA Medical Center at 4815 N Assembly St, Spokane — va.gov .
  • In-Home Urgent Care: DispatchHealth provides same-day medical care at home — dispatchhealth.com .
  • Help Applying for Insurance: Washington Connection at washingtonconnection.org assists with Apple Health enrollment.

Access & Support

  • Call Washington 2-1-1 at (877) 211-9274 for referrals to medical, mental health, and community services.
  • Use the SAMHSA Treatment Locator for additional mental health and substance abuse treatment options.
  • Browse our full Resource Directory for all verified Spokane recovery resources.
