Free Recovery Resources in North Spokane, WA

Connecting North Spokane residents with free, local addiction recovery services.

North Spokane has a strong and supportive recovery community. From Shadle Park to the North Side neighborhoods, Get Sober Spokane helps you find the right resources — whether you need treatment, housing, food support, or just a meeting to attend.

Treatment Resources

Connect with addiction treatment providers serving North Spokane, including counseling, outpatient programs, and peer support.

  • Frontier Behavioral Health — Outpatient and residential services
  • CHAS Health North Spokane — Primary care + addiction medicine
  • SAMHSA Helpline: 1-800-662-4357 (free, 24/7)

Shelter & Housing

Find sober living and transitional housing near North Spokane for people rebuilding after addiction.

  • Truth Ministries Shelter — Emergency beds for men in crisis
  • Union Gospel Mission — Transitional housing and recovery programs
  • SNAP (Spokane Neighborhood Action Partners) — Housing assistance

Sober Activities

North Spokane has plenty of ways to stay active and connected with the sober community.

  • Northside AA meetings — Daily at multiple locations
  • Shadle Park — Walking trails, sports leagues, and outdoor events
  • North Spokane Community Center — Classes and social programs
