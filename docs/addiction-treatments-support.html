Addiction Treatment Centers in Spokane

Spokane offers a wide range of addiction treatment centers providing evidence-based care from medical detox and residential programs to outpatient therapy, medication-assisted treatment (MAT), and specialized youth services. Many centers accept Medicaid, private insurance, and offer sliding-scale fees.
