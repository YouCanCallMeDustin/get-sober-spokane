Support Get Sober Spokane

Your donation helps us provide vital resources and support to those in recovery in the Spokane community. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a real difference.

Why Donate to Get Sober Spokane?

  • Directly supports individuals and families on their recovery journey
  • Funds essential programs: shelter, food, mental health, and employment support
  • Helps us host sober community events and outreach
  • Enables us to provide free resources and referrals to those in need
  • 100% of donations stay local to benefit the Spokane area

"Get Sober Spokane gave me hope and a path forward when I needed it most. Your support truly changes lives."

— Grateful Community Member
