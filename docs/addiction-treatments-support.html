Addiction Treatments & Support Groups in Spokane

Spokane offers a wide range of addiction treatment centers and support groups for individuals and families seeking help with substance use disorders. Services include detox, inpatient and outpatient care, mental health support, and peer-led recovery meetings.

Addiction Treatment Centers

Treatment centers in Spokane provide comprehensive care, from detox and residential programs to outpatient therapy and mental health services. Many offer specialized support for veterans, families, and those experiencing homelessness.

Access & Support

  • Call Washington 2-1-1 at +1 (877) 211-9274 for referrals to addiction treatment, support groups, and other services.
  • Explore the Fig Tree Resource Guide for a comprehensive directory of addiction and recovery resources.
  • Use the SAMHSA Treatment Locator to find additional treatment options.
  • Use the Spokane Resource Google Map to locate treatment centers and support groups.

Need Help with Addiction or Recovery?

Call Washington 2-1-1 or reach out to a local treatment center or support group for immediate assistance.
