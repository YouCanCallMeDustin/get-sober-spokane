Employment & Skills Management in Spokane

Spokane offers a robust network of employment and workforce development resources for adults, youth, seniors, and businesses. Services include job placement, resume help, apprenticeships, and employer support.

Local organizations help job seekers find employment, prepare resumes, and connect with hiring events.

Additional Resources

  • Workforce Wednesday: Weekly segment on Fox 28 at 7:00 AM with updates on workforce services and events ( bitly.spokaneworkforce.org ).
  • workforceNERD. thePODCAST.: Video podcast on workforce development, available through Spokane Workforce Council channels.
  • Spokane’s Educational Institutions: 18 universities and colleges in the region provide a talent pool and training programs ( spokanevalleywa.gov ).

Access & Support

  • Call Washington 2-1-1 at (877) 211-9274 for referrals to employment, training, and community services.
  • Explore the Fig Tree Resource Guide for a comprehensive directory of employment and workforce resources.
  • Use the Spokane Resource Google Map to find employment and training services.
  • Check spokaneworkforce.org for upcoming hiring events and workforce news.

Need Help with Employment or Training?

Call Washington 2-1-1 or connect with a local employment resource for immediate support.
