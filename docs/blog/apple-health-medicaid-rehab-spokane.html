Navigating Apple Health (Medicaid) for Rehab in Washington

How to get your addiction or mental health treatment covered locally.

March 2026  ·  6 min read

"Rehab is too expensive." It is the most common reason people in active addiction hesitate to ask for help. While private, luxury rehabs can cost tens of thousands of dollars, Washington State's Medicaid program—known as **Apple Health**—covers comprehensive substance use disorder treatment for low-income residents. If you have no income, you are likely eligible right now.

What Does Apple Health Cover in Spokane?

Unlike some states, Washington treats addiction as a fundamental medical condition. If a licensed clinician assesses you and determines that treatment is medically necessary, Apple Health covers a vast array of services:

  • Medical Detoxification: Supervised inpatient withdrawal management to keep you safe when detoxing from alcohol, benzos, or severe opioid use.
  • Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP): Group and individual therapy sessions at a local clinic multiple times a week.
  • Residential Inpatient Rehab: Living at a treatment facility for 30, 60, or 90 days.
  • Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT): Full coverage for prescriptions like Suboxone, Vivitrol, or Methadone to treat opioid or alcohol addiction.

Am I Eligible for Apple Health (Medicaid)?

In Washington, Medicaid eligibility for adults (aged 19–64) is simply based on income levels. As of 2025-2026, a single adult making less than approximately $1,732 a month (before taxes) qualifies. If you have children or dependents, that income limit is significantly higher.

**Crucially:** If you are currently unemployed because of your addiction, you qualify immediately.

How to Apply Today

You can apply for Apple Health entirely online. The process usually takes less than 30 minutes.

  • Online at Washington Healthplanfinder: Go to wahealthplanfinder.org and fill out an application. If you meet the income requirements, your coverage often begins on the first day of the month you applied.
  • By Phone: Call the Healthplanfinder Customer Support Center at 1-855-923-4633.
  • In-Person Help in Spokane: If you are overwhelmed or don't have internet access, local organizations like **CHAS Health** employ "Navigators" whose entire job is to sit down with you and help you fill out the application for free. Just walk into a local clinic and ask for a Navigator.

Finding Rehab Centers That Accept Medicaid

Once you are approved, you need to find a "state-certified behavioral health agency" in Spokane that accepts Medicaid (specifically, your Managed Care Organization like Molina, Coordinated Care, or Community Health Plan of WA).

Almost all major community behavioral health centers in Spokane accept Apple Health, including:

  • Frontier Behavioral Health
  • Excelsior Wellness
  • Spokane Regional Health District (Treatment Services)
  • American Behavioral Health Systems (ABHS)
